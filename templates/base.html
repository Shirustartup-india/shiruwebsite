ABOUT US

ENRISSION Inc.



Since many IIT students need connections with new age Japanese investors, Mr. Yusuke Kakimoto launched “SHIRU START-UP “ in 2020 to make a bridge between budding IIT Entrepreneurs and investors from Japan.

We have curated a cafe called “SHIRU START-UP CAFE” in IITs, this is a hub for IIT student entrepreneurs to discuss, get mentorship , host events , co-work and find potential Funding for their start-ups.

In collaboration with entrepreneurship support organisation of IITs called “ E-Cell”, we select the best start-ups and provide connections to Japanese investors and help them raise funds , mentorship and guidance .
